Because of increasing interest in our GeMTC software for network meta-analysis, there is now a GeMTC release announcements mailing list. Subscribers are kept up-to-date on new releases of the GeMTC GUI and R package. We intend to keep the number of messages to a minimum; no more than about 1 per month.
