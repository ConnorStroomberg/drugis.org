--- layout: post title: "ADDIS v0.8 released" date: 2010-04-27 excerpt: categories: news ---

We are happy to announce ADDIS v0.8. ADDIS v0.8 introduces network meta-analysis, making ADDIS the first software to offer automatic model generation for network meta-analysis. In addition, your ADDIS data can now be saved as XML, ensuring that you can still use it in future versions of ADDIS.
