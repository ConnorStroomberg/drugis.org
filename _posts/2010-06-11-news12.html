--- layout: post title: "MTC v0.4" author: gert date: 2010-06-11 excerpt: MTC v0.4 released. categories: news ---

We are happy to announce MTC v0.4, the second release of our library for Mixed Treatment Comparison (network meta-analysis) model generation. It is the first software of it's kind, and can be used to generate correct parametrizations of (in)consistency models for both dichotomous and continuous outcomes.
