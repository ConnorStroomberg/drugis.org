Our manuscript "ADDIS: a decision support system for evidence-based medicine" has been accepted for publication in the Decision Support Systems special issue on "Modeling for Better Healthcare". The paper describes the ADDIS system as such, placing it in the context of clinical development and regulatory assessment. The paper details the data model as well as the evidence synthesis and decision modeling that is supported by ADDIS.
