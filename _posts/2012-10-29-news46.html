--- layout: post title: "GeMTC R package released" author: gert date: 2012-10-29 excerpt: The GeMTC R package 0.1 is now available from CRAN. categories: news ---

The first version (0.1) of the GeMTC R package is now available from CRAN. This version is based on GeMTC 0.14.1 and supports consistency and inconsistency models. Node-splitting models will be added later on.

In addition, the GeMTC pages have been completely rewritten to match the many changes that happened between GeMTC 0.10 and 0.14.
