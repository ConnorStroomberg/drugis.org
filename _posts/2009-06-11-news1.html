--- layout: post title: "drugis.org launched" date: 2009-06-11 excerpt: categories: news ---

drugis.org has been launched to promote the visible and open development of ADDIS, our proof-of-concept information system for the storage, retrieval, (meta-)analysis and benefit-risk assessment of clinical trials data.

The website will contain a description of our software and development process, as well as source code, nightly builds and released versions of our software.
