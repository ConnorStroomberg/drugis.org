--- layout: post title: "ADDIS 1.16.1 and GeMTC 0.14.1 bugfix releases" author: gert date: 2012-10-15 excerpt: Fixes partial saving of GeMTC datasets. categories: news ---

GeMTC 0.14 contained a bug that allowed the GeMTC dataset to be saved only partially. GeMTC 0.14.1 fixes this bug as well as a number of improvements to usability and consistency. These fixes have also been incorporated in ADDIS v1.16.1.
