--- layout: post title: "ADDIS v1.6 released" author: gert date: 2011-04-15 excerpt: ADDIS v1.6 has been released, introducing a new, more formal, data format. categories: news ---

The ADDIS v1.6 release aims to solidify the current functionality and includes many bug fixes and usability enhancements. Moreover, internal improvements have been made to support faster future evolution, such as the introduction of a formal data representation based on XML schema.
